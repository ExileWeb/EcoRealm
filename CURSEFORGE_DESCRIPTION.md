# EcoRealms: Rise & Restore

**A progression-driven survival modpack where your actions heal — or harm — a broken world.**

EcoRealms drops you into a planet scarred by ancient catastrophe. Biomes are unstable, ecosystems are collapsing, and every major advancement has environmental consequences. Build settlements, master technology or nature magic, and restore balance across a living world that reacts to what you do.

---

## 🌍 Core Fantasy
- Rebuild civilization in a damaged world.
- Restore forests, water quality, biodiversity, and climate stability.
- Advance through meaningful progression gates tied to ecosystem recovery.
- Choose your approach: industrial tech, nature magic, or hybrid restoration.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### Dynamic Eco-State Progression
EcoRealms tracks world health through persistent metrics:
- Corruption
- Biodiversity
- Air quality
- Water quality
- Settlement prosperity
- World tier

These values evolve over time and directly influence progression, events, and late-game unlocks.

### Real Consequences, Real Recovery
- Passive decay creates ongoing pressure if you ignore the environment.
- Restoration actions (reforestation, nature support, cleanup loops) improve global conditions.
- Stabilizing the world unlocks stronger progression paths and safer exploration.


### Custom World Generator: Shattered Restoration
- Dedicated **EcoRealms world preset** with custom biome routing.
- Heavy devastation footprint (ashen wastelands + scorched highlands).
- Isolated restoration pockets (`renewal_grove`) that become expansion targets.

### Quest-Driven Narrative Arc
Structured chapters guide your journey from survival to restoration:
1. Ashes of the Old World
2. Roots of Renewal
3. Power With Consequence
4. Paths of Civilization
5. Frontiers and Ruins
6. The Planet Responds
7. Cycle Ascendant

### Tech + Magic + Civilization
- **Tech anchors:** Mekanism, Immersive Engineering, Create ecosystem integration.
- **Magic anchors:** Botania plus nature-focused spell/ritual support.
- **Civilization anchor:** MineColonies-driven settlement growth with environmental stakes.
